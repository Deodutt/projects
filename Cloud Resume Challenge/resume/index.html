About Me

Introduction

My name is Ricardo Deodutt. I was originally born in Guyana but live in New York for most of my life. I am a recent college graduate at CUNY York College with a bachelors degree in Computer Science. I am currently enrolled in a Boot Camp at Kura Labs.

Dreams

At Kura Labs, I am putting in a lot of efforts in hopes of finding a job as a DevOps Engineer. DevOps engineer is a shift in the industry from software engineers. Most companies are also moving towards DevOps because it saves 90% of IT cost. Overall, I enjoy the culture aspect of DevOps. I am a strong believer that developers and operations should work together rather than throwing information back and forth without understanding it. Along with that, DevOps involves using tools to automate tasks which makes going from code to deployment very fast. DevOps also involves using different measurements to understand issues and efficiency. DevOps lastly involves using tools such as Git to share code and make your team aware of what's going on at all times. Overall, DevOps are becoming a new norm from traditional software engineers.
